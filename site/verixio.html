Every Neighborhood, Quantified

Verixio is the rating engine that powers DealScore's neighborhood enrichment layer. It scores any US parcel across three composite dimensions — Nature/Transit/Service, Transport/Communication/Safety, and Vibrancy/Growth/Density.

See the Dimensions Health Check

The Three Dimension Model

Every parcel gets scored on three axes, each composed of sub-factors weighted by their statistical impact on property value and quality of life.

🌿

NTS

Nature / Transit / Service

Proximity to parks, water features, public transit stops, hospitals, fire stations, and everyday services.

Park access · Transit proximity · Hospital & fire station distance · Grocery & retail
🔗

TCS

Transport / Communication / Safety

Highway access, broadband availability, crime rates, and emergency response infrastructure.

Road network density · Broadband coverage · Violent & property crime · EMS response time
📈

VGD

Vibrancy / Growth / Density

Population trends, new construction permits, economic activity indicators, and neighborhood density.

Population growth · Building permits · Commercial density · Employment proximity

How It Works

🏗️ ArcGIS-Powered

Verixio ingests parcel data from ArcGIS and scores each parcel against a weighted composite of ~20 sub-factors drawn from public datasets. The scoring model is deterministic and transparent — every component contributes a known weight.

⚡ FastAPI Backend

Built on FastAPI and deployed on Railway. Returns scores for a parcel or address in milliseconds once parcel data is loaded. The health endpoint confirms service availability.

🔗 DealScore Integration

Verixio runs behind DealScore's neighborhood enrichment pipeline. When DealScore scores a property, it queries Verixio for NTS/TCS/VGD ratings and folds them into the 12-factor risk model. You don't need to call Verixio directly — it's included.
